# The Operating Leader — Brand Guide

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Background:** #0a0a0a (pure dark)
- **Primary text:** #ffffff (white)
- **Accent:** #c9a55a (gold)
- **Muted text:** #888888 (gray)
- **Error/contrast left:** red-tinted for "what most leaders do"
- **Borders/lines:** gold (#c9a55a), thin

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk (bold)
- **Body/labels:** DM Sans (regular/medium)
- **Branding text:** Space Grotesk (small caps, muted gray)

### Visual Formats
1. **2x2 Matrix** — conceptual diagrams, gold lines, white text (1200x1200 square)
2. **Contrast Cards** — split vertical, "What most leaders do" vs "What works" (1200x675 landscape)
3. **Pull Quote Cards** — gold quotation mark, bold quote, author attribution (1200x675 landscape)
4. **Progression Diagrams** — horizontal flow with stages, gold highlight on key stage (1200x675 landscape)

### Design Principles
- Minimal — no icons, no decorations, no gradients
- Dark background always
- Gold used sparingly for emphasis
- Typography-driven, not illustration-driven
- Professional, modern, systems-thinker aesthetic

## Brand Voice
- Direct, opinionated, no fluff
- Operator writing from inside, not coaching from outside
- Frameworks over theory
- "What actually works" > "what you should do"

## Newsletter
- **Name:** The Operating Leader
- **Tagline:** One insight per week on scaling teams from 10 to 100.
- **Platform:** MailerLite (TBD)
- **Format:** One idea per issue, well articulated. Not a link roundup.
